# MoneyWise V8

> A professional browser-based finance research workspace for long-term investors.

MoneyWise V8 is designed to help users research companies, organize investment knowledge, compare businesses, create research notes, and generate structured AI prompts using MoneyBot.

**MoneyWise does NOT provide investment advice.**  
Its purpose is financial education, company research, and knowledge management.

---

# Features

## Dashboard
- Research Workspace
- Market Snapshot
- Quick Statistics
- Recent Activity
- Featured Companies
- Active Research Panel

## Company Research
- Company Overview
- Business Model
- Revenue Sources

- Research Notes

## Workspace
- Favorites
- History

## Compare
Compare multiple companies side-by-side including:

- Business
- Sector
- Type
- Debt
- Cash Flow
- Profitability
- Important Metrics

## MoneyBot

MoneyBot helps generate structured prompts for AI assistants.

Included prompt templates:

- Full Company Research
- Ratio Explanation
- Risk Analysis
- Competitor Comparison
- Beginner Summary

MoneyBot never gives guaranteed returns or buy/sell recommendations.

# Design Philosophy

MoneyWise is designed around five principles.

1. Transparency
2. Source-based research
3. Simple language
4. No financial advice
5. Long-term learning
